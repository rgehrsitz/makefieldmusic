More About Us

Our Services.

Private Violin and Viola lessons with experienced instructors who understand the needs of young beginner and intermediate level students.

Virtual.

We provide lessons via most video chat applications (Zoom, FaceTime, Skype, Teams, etc.). Although differant from in-person lessons, 'virtual' lessons can be just as fun an effective.

In Person.

During the ongoing pandemic, we are still able to provide in-person lessons, but with strict safety measures in place including mandatory mask wearing.
